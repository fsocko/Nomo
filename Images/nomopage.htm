Nomogram

First, enter the parameters for the nomogram into the text fields that appear in the pop-up windows. Once all of the parameters have been entered, you can draw a straight line on the nomogram by clicking and dragging the mouse and then releasing the mouse button. You may re-draw the line as many times as you wish; the line previously drawn will disappear. Values of all three parameters for the line appear at the right.

If this applet does not load, you will need to download version 1.4.1 or higher of JAVA.
